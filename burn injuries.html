BURN INJURY ATTORNEY


Burn injuries are among the most painful injuries someone can experience. Burn injury victims may require costly surgeries, including plastic surgery, and even then, they may be left with permanent scarring. Some burn injury victims suffer from excruciating pain and psychological scars that make it hard for them to ever live a normal life. Because burn injuries are so complex, the effects of your injury can be unpredictable. What may seem like a minor burn can lead to serious complications, such as an aggressive infection impacting multiple parts of your body. Serious burn injuries can require highly specialized treatment in a burn unit along with long term medical care and rehabilitation.

For these reasons, it is important to have an experienced burn injury attorney help you obtain the financial compensation you deserve for your physical and emotional injuries. Whether you think your burn injury is mild or severe, an experienced burn injury attorney can carefully assess your situation and discuss all legal avenues that may be available to you. Depending upon the particular circumstances of your case, multiple parties may be held legally responsible for your injuries, including utility companies, product manufacturers and distributors, building owners, and property management companies. Without the help of a competent burn injury attorney, it may be very difficult to properly investigate and find all sources of potential compensation.

BURN INJURY TREATMENT


Burn injuries can cause damage to your skin, deeper tissues, and even nerves and bones. If you experience a burn injury, it is important to seek medical attention. First degree burns and some second degree burns heal on its own without professional medical treatment. If you suffer a minor burn, run it under cool water immediately and keep it clean to allow it to heal over time.

Third and fourth degree burns are much more serious injuries and must be treated immediately by medical professionals. Sometimes, a person who suffers a severe third or fourth degree burn will not feel pain due to nerve damage. Severe burns, if left untreated, can lead to more disastrous results such as infections, going into shock, and other complicated medical issues. If you cool a large, severe burn too quickly, you may risk getting hypothermia. These injuries cannot be treated at home and require medical attention to reduce and prevent further risks. Deep burn injuries often require skin graft surgery to maximize the healing process.

COMPENSATION FOR BURN INJURY VICTIMS


An experienced Los Angeles burn injury lawyer can help victims collect the compensation they deserve for their injuries and move forward with their lives. Burn injury victims are entitled to receive the following compensation:
Cost of Past and Future Medical Expenses
Loss of Past and Future Income
Loss of Earning Capacity
Pain and Suffering
Emotional Distress
Loss of Enjoyment of Life
Loss of Consortium
Disfigurement
Humiliation

Some important things to consider when evaluating your case is how the burn happened and where the injury is located on your body. Burn injuries can occur in various situations and settings- in the workplace, at home, while using a defective product, etc.
